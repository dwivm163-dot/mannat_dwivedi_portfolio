GENERATIVE ART 003

Experimental Clock

A non-numeric clock that expresses time through visual representation of growth across a lifecycle.

reflection

This was my first time creating a proper animation so it was interesting to see how complicated working with time can be in p5js. I ended up going with a simple flower animation, where time is measured based off how long the flower takes to grow. I think it prompts an interesting way to think about time is terms of life cycles instead of standard units.
